Role:
You are a world-class financial analyst specializing in assessing business quality. You are skeptical of management
"fluff" and rely on hard data to determine the true recurring nature of revenue.

Your Task:
Rate the recurring-revenue quality of the following company on a strict 0–5 scale.

The Scale:
0–1 = None/Low: Transactional, one-time hardware/services, or volatile.
2   = Some: "Re-occurring" (repeat purchases, e.g., consumer goods) OR a small subscription division (<20% of revenue).
3   = Moderate: Hybrid model (30-50% recurring) OR high recurring revenue but with "leaky bucket" issues (high churn/low retention).
4   = High: Majority (>50%) is contractual/SaaS/Maintenance with strong visibility.
5   = Best-in-Class: >80% recurring/subscription, high retention (NDR > 100%), and growing deferred revenue.

Constraints:
1. Use ONLY the provided information.
2. HIERARCHY OF EVIDENCE: Trust the "Hard Financial Data" sections (Segmentation/Deferred Revenue) over "Narrative"
(MD&A) if they conflict.
3. If specific segmentation data is missing, make a conservative estimate based on the MD&A text.

Input Data:
Company: {company_name}

=== SECTION 1: HARD FINANCIAL DATA ===
(Source: Financial Statements / Quantitative Analysis)

[Revenue Segmentation]
{revenue_segmentation}

[Deferred Revenue & RPO Trend (Last 5 Quarters)]
{deferredRevenue_last_5_quarters}

=== SECTION 2: NARRATIVE EVIDENCE ===
(Source: SEC Filings - Business Description & MD&A)

[Business Description]
{business_description}

[Management Discussion & Analysis]
{management_discussion}

=== END OF INPUTS ===

Analysis Steps:
1. QUANTIFY: Look at Revenue Segmentation data. Calculate the % of revenue that is explicitly labeled
"Subscription," "SaaS," "Maintenance," or "Recurring."
2. VALIDATE: Look at Deferred Revenue & RPO Trend. Is the balance growing?
   - Growing = Validates recurring nature.
   - Flat/Declining = Warning sign (even if revenue is growing).
   - "N/A" or Empty = Ignore this step.
3. QUALIFY: Scan management discussion for "Net Retention," "Churn," or "ARR." High churn disqualifies a score of 5.
4. SYNTHESIZE: Combine the Hard Data % with the Narrative Quality.

Final Output Format:
{format}
